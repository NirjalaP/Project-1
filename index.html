Featured Animal

African Lion - King of the Savanna

Scientific Name: Panthera leo
Habitat: African Savanna
Diet: Carnivore
Lifespan: 10-14 years (wild)

Meet Simba, our magnificent male African lion. Known for his impressive mane and powerful presence, Simba serves as an ambassador for his species, helping us educate visitors about big cat conservation. African lions are social animals that live in groups called prides, and are known for their distinctive roar that can be heard up to 5 miles away!
